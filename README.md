#### Activity 3.1
This Project was about figuring out Excel and being able to find different products from different online websites to compare and contrast them. 
![Screen Shot 2026-04-21 at 7.52.34 PM.png](assets/Screen%20Shot%202026-04-21%20at%207.52.34%20PM.png)
In this activity, I explored a variety of technology products using online retail and information websites such as BestBuy, Back Market, and GSMArena. The goal was to observe differences in specifications, pricing, operating systems, and product categories across common devices.I looked at Office 365 and Photoshop to understand pricing and packaging models, then explored Chromebooks, desktops, and laptops to compare specifications such as storage, RAM, processor type, and price. I also reviewed tablets, identifying iPadOS, Android, and Windows as the main operating systems, and examined monitors, smartphones, and printers to observe how features like screen quality, performance, and brand affect cost. Additionally, I analyzed why smartphone prices vary widely based on features like cameras, storage, and technology. Finally, I compared all-in-one computers with and without touch screens. Overall, the activity helped me understand how specifications and features influence technology pricing and consumer choices.
